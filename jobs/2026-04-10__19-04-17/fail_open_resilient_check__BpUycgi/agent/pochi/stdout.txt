}

### `resilient_check.js`
This module exports the two functions: `resilientCheck` and `resilientCheckStrict`.
- It initializes `autumn` and `autumnStrict` using the provided `AUTUMN_SECRET_KEY`.
- Both instances are initialized with `failOpen: false` to disable the SDK's built-in fail-open mechanism. This allows the wrapper to catch network/5xx errors manually, log `"AUTUMN UNREACHABLE, FAILING OPEN"` to `stderr`, and return `{ allowed: true, source: 'fail-open' }` as requested.
- `resilientCheckStrict` simply re-throws errors since there is no `try/catch` block wrapping it.
- *Note:* In newer versions of the `autumn-js` SDK, `.check` is located on the root client rather than `.customers`. To strictly adhere to your instruction to call `autumn.customers.check`, I added a lightweight proxy binding the method to `.customers` to ensure compatibility.
